Privacy Policy for FindMyLink

Last updated: July 4, 2025

FindMyLink is a Chrome extension that helps users search and manage their open tabs and saved bookmarks.

What data we collect

FindMyLink does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing activity. All data such as bookmarks, tabs, and extension settings is accessed locally through Chrome APIs and remains on your device.

Permissions used

  • bookmarks: Used to search and display your bookmarks.
  • tabs: Used to search, activate, or close open tabs.
  • storage: Used to save extension settings locally on your device.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All functionality happens locally in your browser.

Third-party APIs

If you subscribe via Telegram, your Telegram ID is used solely to manage your license. No browsing data is ever sent to external services.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
